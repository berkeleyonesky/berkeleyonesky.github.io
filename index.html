Creating Safe Educational Environments for Foster Children

At UC Berkeley, we understand that safety, stability, and positivity are prerequisites for learning and intellectual curiousity. That's why we reached out to A Better Way to further their mission of empowering foster families by sharing our knowledge from subjects ranging from STEM to the humanities

With members from various fields of study, A Better Way at Berkeley offers dedicated, personalized mentoring to help children of all ages succeed academically.
