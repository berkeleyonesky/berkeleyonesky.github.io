What We Do

Our mission is to provide support, predominantly educational, to children and families relating to the foster care system. To do so, we focus on providing qualified tutors, providing quality instruction in a positive and healty environment.

To do so, we work for the non-profit parent organization A Better Way to support foster families. Our major roles include:

  1. One-on-One long-term tutoring across numerous subjects.
  2. Recruitment Events e.g. Street Fairs/Events in the Counties we serve (Alameda, Contra Costa, Solano and San Francisco).
  3. Help Holiday/Social Events e.g. Holiday Party 12/7/2019, possibly a spring picnic.

A Better Way

Founded here in the City of Berkeley, parent organization A Better Way "is a San Francisco Bay Area 501(c)(3) nonprofit that empowers children and families to develop the insights, life skills, and permanent relationships that promote their social, emotional, and economic well-being."
